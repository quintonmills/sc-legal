PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

ScoreKings ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

To provide a secure Daily Fantasy Sports experience, we collect the following:

2.1 Personal Identifiers

  • Account Data: Name, email address, date of birth, and phone number.
  • Identity Verification: We may collect Social Security Numbers (SSN) for tax compliance (Form 1099-MISC) if winning thresholds are met.

2.2 Financial Information

  • Payment Data: We use third-party processors (Apple Pay/Stripe). We do not store full credit card numbers.
  • Transaction History: Records of contest entries and payouts.

2.3 Location Data

  • Geo-fencing: We collect precise GPS coordinates to verify you are in a legal jurisdiction for real-money play.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Verify identity and age (18+).
  • Ensure legal compliance via geo-fencing.
  • Process financial transactions.
  • Prevent fraud and multi-accounting.

4. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access your data or delete your account at any time via the App Settings. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.
