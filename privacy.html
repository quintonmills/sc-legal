PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

ScoreKings ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application and related services. By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

To provide a secure Daily Fantasy Sports experience, we collect the following categories of data:

2.1 Personal Identifiers

  • Account Data: Name, email address, date of birth, and phone number.
  • Government ID: For tax compliance (Form 1099-MISC) and identity verification, we may collect Social Security Numbers (SSN) for users who exceed certain winning thresholds.

2.2 Financial Information

  • Payment Data: We use third-party payment processors (Apple Pay/Stripe) to handle deposits and withdrawals. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Transaction History: We maintain records of contest entries, entry fees, and payouts.

2.3 Precise Location Data (Required for Compliance)

  • Geo-fencing: As a real-money competition app, we are legally required to verify your physical location. We collect precise GPS coordinates to ensure participation is restricted to legal jurisdictions.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected data to:

  • Verify your identity and age (18+).
  • Ensure compliance with state-specific Daily Fantasy Sports laws via geo-fencing.
  • Process financial transactions and contest payouts.
  • Prevent fraud, multi-accounting, and "cheating."
  • Communicate with you regarding account updates or customer support.

4. DATA SHARING & THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Payment Processors to facilitate wallet transactions.
  • Identity Verification Services to confirm user eligibility.
  • Regulatory/Legal Authorities if required by law or to report tax earnings.

5. DATA RETENTION & SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption and secure hashing) to protect your data. We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to comply with legal/tax obligations.

6. YOUR RIGHTS & CHOICES

6.1 Access and Correction

You may review or change your account information by logging into the App.

6.2 Account Deletion (Apple Requirement)

You have the right to delete your account and all associated personal data at any time. This can be done directly within the App Settings. Once deleted, this action is irreversible, and any remaining balance will be handled according to our Terms and Conditions.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are strictly prohibited for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under this age. If we become aware of an account belonging to a minor, it will be terminated immediately.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
