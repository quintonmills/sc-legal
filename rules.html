SKILL-BASED RULES

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Legal Notice: ScoreKings is a platform for skill-based analytical contests. Contests are won based on the user's knowledge of sports statistics and performance projections. These are not games of chance.

1. SKILL-BASED DETERMINATION

Every contest on ScoreKings requires the application of skill, including:

  • Statistical Analysis: Evaluating historical player performance data.
  • Environmental Factors: Considering injuries, weather, and matchups.
  • Strategy: Selecting a balanced roster or projection set to maximize points.

2. CONTEST FORMAT

Users enter contests by submitting analytical "entries" based on player performance projections. Points are awarded based on the accuracy of these projections relative to real-world outcomes.

3. REWARDS & CREDITS

ScoreKings operates on a Credit-Based System to ensure compliance with regional regulations:

  • Earning Credits: High-performing entries earn "Credits."
  • Non-Monetary Value: Credits are not legal tender and cannot be withdrawn as cash.
  • Redemption: Credits may be redeemed in the Rewards Store for third-party Gift Cards (e.g., Amazon, Apple).

4. ELIGIBILITY & GEORGIA COMPLIANCE

In accordance with Georgia state law and "Underdog-style" operational flows:

  • Users must be 18+ years of age.
  • Users must be physically located in a permitted jurisdiction (verified via geo-fencing).
  • Outcome is determined strictly by the statistical performance of athletes, not by point spreads or the final score of a single team.

5. NO CHANCE INVOLVED

Contests are won by the participants who exercise the highest degree of skill. If a contest results in a tie, the platform utilizes pre-defined tie-breaking logic based on secondary statistical performance metrics.

Contact: For detailed scoring rubrics or specific contest questions, please contact support via the App Settings.
